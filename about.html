About Agoo

History, culture, and community

Our History

Agoo was founded in 1578 and is one of the oldest towns in La Union.

It became a trading center and religious hub during Spanish times.

Today, it preserves its heritage while embracing modern growth.

Cultural Heritage

  • Local crafts
  • Ilocano cuisine
